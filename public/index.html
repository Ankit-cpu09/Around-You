Process

How It Works

From registration to payment — a seamless workflow in four simple steps.

01

Sign Up

Register as an Employer or Worker. Your GPS location is captured automatically for nearby job matching.

02

Post or Find Jobs

Employers post jobs with details and budget. Workers receive instant real-time notifications on their dashboard.

03

Accept & Navigate

Workers accept a job with one click and use integrated Google Maps to navigate directly to the job site.

04

Get Paid

After job completion, employers pay through the secure checkout. Workers get an instant payment confirmation.
